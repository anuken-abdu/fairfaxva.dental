The Clear Alternative to Braces

Invisalign uses a series of invisible, removable, and comfortable aligners that no one can tell you're wearing. So, you can smile more during treatment as well as after.

Benefits:

  • Invisible: Clear aligners are virtually undetectable.
  • Removable: Eat and drink what you want during treatment.
  • Comfortable: No metal brackets or wires to cause mouth irritation.
