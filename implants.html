A Permanent Solution for Missing Teeth

Dental implants are the gold standard for tooth replacement. Unlike dentures, which can slip and cause discomfort, implants are anchored securely into your jawbone, functioning exactly like natural tooth roots.

Why Choose Implants?

  • Natural Look & Feel: Implants differ from other replacements because they function like your own teeth.
  • Bone Health: They are the only restoration option that preserves natural bone and stimulates bone growth.
  • Durability: With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime.

The Procedure

The process involves placing a titanium post into the jawbone. Over a few months, this post fuses with the bone (osseointegration). Once healed, a custom-crafted crown is attached, completing your smile.
