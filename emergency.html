Immediate Relief When You Need It

Dental emergencies can happen at any time. Whether it's a severe toothache, a knocked-out tooth, or a broken crown, our team is ready to provide urgent care to relieve pain and save your smile.

Common Emergencies We Treat:

  • Severe toothache or abscess.
  • Chipped or broken teeth.
  • Knocked-out or loosened teeth.
  • Lost fillings or crowns.
  • Soft tissue injuries.
